Disconnect from QuickBooks

Momentum Health Dashboard

The Momentum Health Dashboard is a private, single-user tool. If you want to revoke its access to your QuickBooks Online data, you can do so at any time. There are two ways, and either is sufficient.

Option 1 — From QuickBooks Online

  1. Sign in at qbo.intuit.com.
  2. Click the gear icon ⚙️ (top right) → Apps.
  3. Find the Momentum Health Dashboard app in the list.
  4. Click Disconnect.

Option 2 — From Google Apps Script

  1. Open your QBO Sync Apps Script project.
  2. Select the resetAuth function from the function dropdown.
  3. Click Run ▶.
  4. Stored OAuth tokens and automatic triggers are cleared immediately.

After disconnecting

The Dashboard can no longer read balance or transaction data from QuickBooks. Previously retrieved data remains in your private Google Sheet and browser local storage until you choose to delete it. See the Privacy Policy for details on what is stored and where.
