About Dr. Holzinger

Finding the Why Behind
Your Symptoms

Dr. Weston Holzinger is a Chiropractic physician with advanced training in nutrition and functional biochemistry. He graduated from Logan University as a Doctor of Chiropractic with honors and earned a Master's degree in Nutrition and Human Performance as valedictorian — receiving multiple awards including the Clinical Science Award.

His passion is to uncover the why behind a patient's symptoms and guide them not just toward being symptom-free, but toward continually growing in health.

His primary treatment methods include manual adjustments, myofascial therapies, nutritional analysis, lifestyle recommendations, and acupuncture/pressure — blending Eastern principles with the Western scientific model.
