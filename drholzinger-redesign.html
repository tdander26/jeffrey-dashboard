About Dr. Holzinger

Finding the Why Behind
Your Symptoms

Dr. Holzinger is a Chiropractic physician with advanced training in nutrition and functional biochemistry. He graduated from Logan University as a Doctor of Chiropractic with honors and earned his Master's degree in Nutrition and Human Performance as valedictorian, receiving the Clinical Science Award among others.

His treatment methods include manual adjustments, myofascial therapies, nutritional analysis, lifestyle recommendations, and acupuncture — blending Eastern principles with the Western scientific model to get at the real root of your problem.
